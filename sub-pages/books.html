BOOKS

The Skin We’re In by Desmond Cole

- Canadian author exposes the racist actions of the Toronto police force, “detailing the dozens of times he had been stopped and interrogated under the controversial practice of carding”. Brings focus onto injustices black Canadians face

How To Be An Antiracist by Ibram X. Kendi

- Memoir that focuses on the concept of antiracism and what an antiracist society might look like

White Fragility by Robin DiAngelo

- Explores “counterproductive reactions white people have when their assumptions about race are challenged, and how these reactions maintain racial inequality

So You Want To Talk About Race by Ijeoma Oluo

- Puts on display aspects of white supremacy: police brutality, mass incarceration of African Americans
