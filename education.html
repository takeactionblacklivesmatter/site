EDUCATION IS KEY TO UNDERSTANDING

The Black Lives Matter movement has been asking for the attention of the world for long enough. They have been asking that everyone learns about the racist acts committed against African American for centuries in America, and the laws that continue to discriminate to this day. One cannot truly understand what they suffer from, but educating oneself is the first step to understanding their plight. Learn about these and more by watching films and documentaries, reading books, or listening to educational podcasts. Netflix also has its own collection of over 40 different films, shows, and documentaries about the black lives matter movement.
